## [v1.1.0] - 2022-02-27 ##

[v1.1.0]: https://github.com/mfederczuk/typesafe-array-js/releases/tags/v1.1.0

### Changed ###

### Security ##

* Updated dependencies

## [v1.0.0] - 2020-04-10 ##

[v1.0.0]: https://github.com/mfederczuk/typesafe-array-js/releases/tags/v1.0.0

### Added ###

* 9 types:
  * `custom`
  * `any`
  * `unknown`
  * `bigint`
  * `boolean`
  * `number`
  * `string`
  * `object`
  * `instance`
* 4 dimensions (0 - 3)
